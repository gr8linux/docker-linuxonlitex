FP Natural Logarithm

Information

Type: FP Natural Logarithm
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The Gowin FP Natural Logarithm IP can get the natural logarithm of a single-precision format number. The IP also supports output ports zero and nan.
