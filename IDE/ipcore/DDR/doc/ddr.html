DDR

Information

Type: DDR
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

Through this IP, users can produce a DDR, which is implemented by the combination of IOLOGICs, IOBUF, IODELAY and other primitives. DDR can be configured in different modes, including Input, Output, Tristate and Bidirectional modes. Users can also specify the Data Width, Ratio, and whether to use IODELAY and CLKDIV.
