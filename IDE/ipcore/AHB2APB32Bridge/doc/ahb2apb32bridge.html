AHB to APB 32 Bridge

Information

Type: AHB to APB 32 Bridge
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The Gowin AHB to APB 32 Bridge IP is used for converting transactions between an AHB bus and an APB bus. The bridge is compliant with AMBA 4 specifications. It is a slave on the AHB bus and is the only master on the APB bus.
