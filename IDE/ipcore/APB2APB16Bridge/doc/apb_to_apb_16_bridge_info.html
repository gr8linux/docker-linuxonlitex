APB to APB 16 Bridge

Information

Type: APB to APB 16 Bridge
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The Gowin APB to APB 16 Bridge IP is used for converting transactions from an APB bus to 16 APB buses. The bridge is compliant with AMBA 3 specifications. It works as 16 slaves on the APB bus.
