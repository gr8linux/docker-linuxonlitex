ADC

Information

Type: ADC
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The Arora V series ADC provides analog delta sigma modulators to meet the needs of users for temperature detection and voltage detection in multiple areas within the chip. The Arora V series ADC is equipped with a high-precision reference voltage source, which has the characteristics of low power consumption and high accuracy in temperature and power voltage detection.
