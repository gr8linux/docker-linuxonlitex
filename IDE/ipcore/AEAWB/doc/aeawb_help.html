AEAWB

Information

Type: AEAWB
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor
Summary: The Gowin AEAWB IP is used for auto exposure and auto white balance. The IP collects the statistical information of the image, calculates the required gain, and outputs the adjusted image.

The design has these features:

  • Support bayer image
  • Support resolutions from 8 × 8 up to 4320 × 7680
  • Support 8,10,12,16-bits per pixel input and output
  • Support outputs for R,G,B,Y channel average
