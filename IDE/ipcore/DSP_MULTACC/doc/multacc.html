MULTACC

Information

Type: MULTACC
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The MULTACC can implement the sum of three multipliers, accumulation and cascading capabilities. The MULTACC can be configured to work in the following operation modes:

  • (DATAIN0*COFFIN0)+(DATAIN1*COFFIN1)+(DATAIN2*COFFIN2)
  • CASI+(DATAIN0*COFFIN0)+(DATAIN1*COFFIN1)+(DATAIN2*COFFIN2)
  • Accum+(DATAIN0*COFFIN0)+(DATAIN1*COFFIN1)+(DATAIN2*COFFIN2)
