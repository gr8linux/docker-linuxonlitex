DVI TX

Information

Type: DVI TX
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor
Summary: The DVI TX IP is used to transmit the video timing signals and data into Transition Minimized Differential Signaling(TMDS), and transmit it over the Digital Visual Interface(DVI). In DVI, three channels are designated for red, green, and blue color components(RGB888) in each video pixel data.

Options & Description

Clock Setting

Using External Clock :

  • The choice of whether using external clock.

TX Clock In Frequency :

  • The pixel clock frequency of DVI TX.

IO Setting

OBUF Type :

  • The choice of OBUF Type, TLVDS_OBUF or ELVDS_OBUF.
