DVI TX

Information

Type: DVI TX
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The DVI TX IP is used to transmit the video timing signals and data into Transition Minimized Differential Signaling(TMDS), and transmit it over the Digital Visual Interface(DVI). In DVI, three channels are designated for red, green, and blue color components(RGB888) in each video pixel data.
