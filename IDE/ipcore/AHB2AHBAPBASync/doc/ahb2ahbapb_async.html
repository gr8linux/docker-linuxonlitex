AHB to AHB APB Async

Information

Type: AHB to AHB APB Async
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The Gowin AHB to AHB APB Async IP is used for interconnection between AHB devices and AHB devices or between AHB devices and APB devices. The Gowin AHB to AHB APB Async IP can configure the Master Width as 1-4.
