FP Div

Information

Type: FP Div
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The Gowin FP Div IP can get the quotient of two single-precision format numbers. The IP supports optional output ports such as overflow, underflow, zero, nan, and division_by_zero.
