MIPI_DPHY_RX

Information

Type: MIPI_DPHY_RX
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The Arora V devices provide a standalone MIPI D-PHY RX supporting MIPI Alliance Standard for D-PHY Specification V2.1. The D-PHY core supports MIPI DSI and CSI-2 mobile video interfaces for cameras and displays.
