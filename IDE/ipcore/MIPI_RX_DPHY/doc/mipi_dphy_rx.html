MIPI_DPHY_RX

Information

Type: MIPI_DPHY_RX
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

Gowin IPCore MIPI_DPHY_RX is defined in GW1N-2/GW1NR-2/GW1NZ-2. Compliant to MIPI D-PHY v1.2. The IPCore that supports flowing features: speeds up to 1.5 Gbps per lane of RX and 6.0Gbps per quad, one quad with 1 unidirectional clock lane and up to 4 unidirectional data lanes for RX; Automatic termination dynamic control in high-speed and low-power modes; MIPI D-PHY HS-RX lane signaling, data rate of 80-1500Mbps; MIPI D-PHY LP-RX and LP-TX lane signaling, data rate up to 10Mbps; Word align at lane level; RX lane HS de-skew function.
