Advanced FIR Filter

Information

Type: Advanced FIR Filter
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The Gowin Advanced FIR Filter IP implements the FIR filter function. According to different types of FIR filters, it supports 4 mode configurations, and supports configurations of functions such as multi-channel processing and time division multiplexing processing.
