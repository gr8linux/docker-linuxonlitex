SDI Decoder

Information

Type: SDI Decoder
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

Serial digital interface (SDI) is a member of the family of digital video interfaces, it can be used for transmitting digital video signals. Gowin SDI Decoder IP can operate under the HD or 3G rate standard defined by the Society of Motion Picture and Television Engineers (SMPTE), is used to transmit the SDI signals to the video timing signals and data.
