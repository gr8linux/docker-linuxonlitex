AHB to AHB 16 Bridge

Information

Type: AHB to AHB 16 Bridge
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The Gowin AHB to AHB 16 Bridge IP is used for converting transactions from an AHB bus to 16 AHB buses. The bridge is compliant with AMBA 3 specifications. It works as 16 slaves on the AHB bus.
