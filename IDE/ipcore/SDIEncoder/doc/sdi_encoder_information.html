SDI Encoder

Information

Type: SDI Encoder
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

Serial digital interface (SDI) is a member of the family of digital video interfaces, it can be used for transmitting digital video signals. Gowin SDI Encoder IP can operate under the HD or 3G rate standard defined by the Society of Motion Picture and Television Engineers (SMPTE), is used to transmit the video timing signals and data to the SDI signals.
