UART MASTER

Information

Type: UART MASTER
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The Gowin UART Master IP is a UART Master controller with synchronous SRAM interface, and the external connection standard RS-232 interface. Data is converted between serial communication and parallel communication.
