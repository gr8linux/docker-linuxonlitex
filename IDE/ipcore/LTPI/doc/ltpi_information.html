DC-SCM LTPI

Information

Type: DC-SCM LTPI
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

Gowin DC-SCM LTPI IP is a protocol and interface designed for tunneling various low-speed signals between HPM and SCM. The LTPI protocol goes over the LVDS (Low Voltage Differential Signals) electrical interfaces supported by majority of the CPLDs and FPGAs. It allows for tunneling of not only GPIOs but also low speed serial interfaces such as I2C and UART. It is also extensible with additional proprietary OEM interfaces and provides support for raw Data tunneling between HPM CPLD and SCM CPLD.
