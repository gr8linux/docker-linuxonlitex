Scaler Lite Down

Information

Type: Scaler Lite Down
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor
Summary: The Scaler Lite Down IP is used to convert the input video frames from one size down to another size. It only supports bilinear algorithm and 2:1, 3:2, 4:3 zoom ratio.
