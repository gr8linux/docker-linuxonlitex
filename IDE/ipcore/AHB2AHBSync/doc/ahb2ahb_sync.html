AHB to AHB Sync

Information

Type: AHB to AHB Sync
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The Gowin AHB to AHB Sync IP is used for interconnection between AHB devices and AHB devices. The Gowin AHB to AHB Sync IP can configure the Master Width as 1-4.
