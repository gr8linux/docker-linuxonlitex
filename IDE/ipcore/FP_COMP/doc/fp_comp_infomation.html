FP Comp

Information

Type: FP Comp
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The Gowin FP Comp IP can compare two single precision floating point data. The IP supports customize the output ports of A=B, A!=B, A>B, A>=B, A<B, A<=B and Unorder.
