DCS

Information

Type: DCS
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

Gowin FPGA provides a dynamic clock selector (DCS), which is used for dynamic switching of clocks. DCS has two clock switching modes, Non-glitchless and glitchless mode. In Non-glitchless mode, the DCS acts like a conventional multiplexer, switching clock signals though the CLKSEL signal. In glitchless mode, the clock signals switched by parameter DCS_MODE and signal CLKSEL.
