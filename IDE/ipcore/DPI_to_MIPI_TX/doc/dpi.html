DPI to MIPI TX

Information

Type: DPI to MIPI TX
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The Gowin DPI to MIPI TX IP coverts DPI parallel interface(RGB666 or RGB888) to MIPI TX interface (Gowin MIPI TX Advance IP).
