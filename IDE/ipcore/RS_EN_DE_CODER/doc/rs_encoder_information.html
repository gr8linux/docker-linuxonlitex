Reed-Solomon Encoder

Information

Type: Reed-Solomon Encoder
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The Gowin Reed-Solomon Encoder IP is used in many Forward Error Correction applications and communications systems. In an error correction system, the encoder adds check symbols to the data stream prior to its transmission over a communications channel.
