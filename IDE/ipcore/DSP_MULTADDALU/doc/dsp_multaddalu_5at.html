MULTADDALU

Information

Type: MULTADDALU
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The MULTADDALU can implement the sum of two 12x12 multipliers, accumulation and cascading capabilities. The MULTADDALU can be configured to work in the following operation modes:

  • +/-(A0*B0)+/-(A1*B1)
  • CASI+/-(A0*B0)+/-(A1*B1)
  • Accum+/-(A0*B0)+/-(A1*B1)
  • Accum+CASI+/-(A0*B0)+/-(A1*B1)
