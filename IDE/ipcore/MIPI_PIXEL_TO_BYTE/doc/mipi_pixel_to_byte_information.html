MIPI Pixel-to-Byte Converter

Information

Type: MIPI Pixel-to-Byte Converter
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

MIPI D-PHY is designed to replace traditional parallel video buses based on LVCMOS or LVDS. However, many processors, monitors, or cameras still use these legacy interfaces. To connect to MIPI D-PHY TX IP, these traditional parallel video formats must be converted to a MIPI D-PHY compatible byte stream format.

This MIPI Pixel-to-Byte IP is used to convert standard parallel video formats into the desired MIPI DSI or CSI-2 byte stream format.
