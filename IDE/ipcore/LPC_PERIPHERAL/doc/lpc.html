LPC Peripheral

Information

Type: LPC Peripheral
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The Gowin LPC Peripheral IP is used to connect LPC bus as peripheral around the CPU and to replace the ISA bus. It can be transmitted across seven lines.

The design has these features:

  • Support LPC Peripheral
  • Support seven lines
  • Support I/O read and write
  • Support insert SYNC Phase to keep wait states
