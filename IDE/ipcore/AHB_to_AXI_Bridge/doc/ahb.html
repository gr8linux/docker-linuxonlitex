AHB to AXI Bridge IP

Information

Type: AHB to AXI Bridge
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The Gowin AHB to AXI Bridge IP is used for converting transactions between an AHB-Lite bus and an AXI4/AXI4-Lite bus. The AHB-Lite port is compliant with AMBA 3 specification. The AXI4/AXI4-Lite port is compliant with AMBA 4 specification. It works as a slave on the AHB-Lite bus and as a master on the AXI4/AXI4-Lite bus.
