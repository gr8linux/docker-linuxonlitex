MULT

Information

Type: MULT
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The MULT includes two functions: multiplication and multiplication based on pre-addition. The multiplication function refers to A×B, and the multiplication based on pre-addition function refers to (A+/-D)×B.
