MULTALU

Information

Type: MULTALU
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The MULTALU includes pre-addition, multiplication and ALU three functions, and can be configured as 27x18 mode. The MULTALU can implement Multiplication, pre-addition, accumulation and cascading capabilities. The MULTALU can be configured to work in the following operation modes:

  • +/-(A*B)+/-C
  • +/-((A+/-D)*B)+/-C
  • Accum+/-(A*B)
  • Accum+/-((A+/-D)*B)
  • Accum+/-(A*B)+/-C
  • Accum+/-((A+/-D)*B)+/-C
  • CASI+/-(A*B)
  • CASI+/-((A+/-D)*B)
  • CASI+/-(A*B)+/-C
  • CASI+/-((A+/-D)*B)+/-C
  • CASI+Accum+/-(A*B)
  • CASI+Accum+/-((A+/-D)*B)
  • CASI+Accum+/-(A*B)+/-C
  • CASI+Accum+/-((A+/-D)*B)+/-C
