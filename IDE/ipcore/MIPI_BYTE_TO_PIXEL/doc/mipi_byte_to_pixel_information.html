MIPI Byte-to-Pixel Converter

Information

Type: MIPI Byte-to-Pixel Converter
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The MIPI Byte-to-Pixel Converter IP converts DSI/CSI-2 video payload packets to standard pixel data format, and also generates video synchronization signals based on the packet header information.
