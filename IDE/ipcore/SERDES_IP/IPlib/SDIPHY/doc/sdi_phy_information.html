SDI PHY

Information

Type: SDI PHY
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

Serial digital interface (SDI) is a member of the family of digital video interfaces, it can be used for transmitting digital video signals. Gowin SDI PHY IP can operate under the HD or 3G rate standard defined by the Society of Motion Picture and Television Engineers (SMPTE), and can act as a transmitter and receiver at the same time.
