JESD204B

Information

Type: JESD204B
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

JESD204B is a high-speed serial interface designed to connect Analog-to-Digital Converter(ADCs) and Digital-to-Analog Converter (DACs) to logic devices. The JESD204B interface is specified in the JEDEC JESD204B Specification.
