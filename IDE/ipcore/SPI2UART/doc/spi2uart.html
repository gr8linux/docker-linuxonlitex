SPI_UART

Information

Type: SPI_UART
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

SPI_UART is a high performance one channel universal asynchronous receiver and transmitter (UART) with 64 byte TX and RX FIFOs and a SPI slave interface.
