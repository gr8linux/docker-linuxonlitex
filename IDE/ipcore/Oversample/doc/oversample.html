OverSample

Information

Type: OverSample
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

Gowin OverSample can sample 4 times in one high speed clock cycle, and provides two types of oversampling Deserializer, including 1 to 32 oversampling Deserializer(OSIDES32) and 1 to 64 oversampling Deserializer(OSIDES64). OSIDES32 achieves 1 to 32 oversampling function through two sampling lanes, and OSIDES64 achieves 1 to 64 oversampling function through four sampling lanes.
