DVI RX

Information

Type: DVI RX
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The DVI RX IP is used to receive Transition Minimized Differential Signaling(TMDS) over the Digital Visual Interface(DVI), and transmit it into the video timing signals and data. In DVI, three channels are designated for red, green, and blue color components(RGB888) in each video pixel data.
