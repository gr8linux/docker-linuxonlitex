LICD

Information

Type: LICD
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

The Gowin Lossless Image Compress and Decompress (LICD) is specified for the application of real-time lossless image compression codec.

LICD includes an encoder and symmetric decoder with following main features:

  • The average compression ratio is 25%~70% with low latency
  • Line to line independent coder and decoder
  • Support different image color spaces including RGB, YCbCr, etc.
  • High throughput up to 4k@60Hz 8-bit RGB or YCbCr444 video
