SDRAM Controller HS

Information

Type: SDRAM Controller HS
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

High speed SDRAM Controller. This SDRAM Controller HS IP is an enhanced design on top of previous SDRAM Controller (with embedded SDRAM or with external SDRAM) with improvement on design architect & speed. It can interface either embedded or external SDRAM.
