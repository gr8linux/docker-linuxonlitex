PADD

Information

Type: PADD
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

Each gwDSP macro features two units of pre-adders at the first stage located before two multipliers. The pre-adder accepts two inputs. One is parallel 18-bit input B or Backward shift input, and the other is either parallel 18-bit input A or Shift input.
