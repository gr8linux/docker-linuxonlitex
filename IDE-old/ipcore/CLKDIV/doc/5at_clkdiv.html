CLKDIV

Information

Type: CLKDIV
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

Gowin FPGA provides a clock division module (CLKDIV), which is used for high-speed clock division. The division factors include 1,2,3,3.5,4,5,6,7 and 8.
