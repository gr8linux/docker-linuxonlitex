Complex Multiplier

Information

Type: Complex Multiplier
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor

Summary

Based on the needs of complex multiplication width, the Gowin Complex Multiplier IP can be configured as 9x9, 18x18 or 36x36 mode. The Gowin Complex Multiplier IP which is configured as 9x9 mode contains 4 MULT9X9 and 2 ALU54D. The Gowin Complex Multiplier IP which is configured as 18x18 mode contains 2 MULTADDALU18X18. The Gowin Complex Multiplier IP which is configured as 36X36 mode contains 4 MULT36X36 and 2 ALU54D.
